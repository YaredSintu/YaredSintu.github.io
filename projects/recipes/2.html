Tibs

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb beef or lamb, cut into small cubes
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, minced
  • 1 teaspoon berbere spice blend
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh rosemary or thyme (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the onions and saut√© until they are golden brown.
  3. Add the garlic and ginger, and cook for another minute.
  4. Add the meat and cook until browned on all sides.
  5. Add the tomatoes, bell pepper, berbere spice, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  6. Cover and simmer for about 20-30 minutes, or until the meat is tender.
  7. If using, add fresh rosemary or thyme during the last 5 minutes of cooking.
  8. Serve hot with injera.
