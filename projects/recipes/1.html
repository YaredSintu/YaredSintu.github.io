Doro Wat

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken drumsticks or thighs, skin removed
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 3 tablespoons berbere spice blend
  • 1/4 cup niter kibbeh (Ethiopian spiced clarified butter) or vegetable oil
  • 1 cup chicken broth or water
  • 4 hard-boiled eggs, peeled (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the niter kibbeh or oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until they are golden brown, about 15-20 minutes.
  3. Add the garlic and ginger, and cook for another minute.
  4. Add the berbere spice blend and stir well to coat the onions.
  5. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are browned on all sides.
  6. Pour in the chicken broth or water, bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover and simmer for about 45 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  7. If using, add the hard-boiled eggs during the last 10 minutes of cooking to heat them through.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve hot with injera or rice.
