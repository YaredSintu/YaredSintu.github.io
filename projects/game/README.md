One Piece: East Blue to Alabasta


The game takes place in the world of One Piece. You play as Monkey D. Luffy, a pirate with a rubber body, starting from a barrel in the ocean and traveling through the East Blue to the Grand Line. The final battle takes place in the desert kingdom of Alabasta.

Story Summary

You burst out of a barrel and immediately have to fight the pirate Alvida. After beating her, you travel from island to island. On some islands, you recruit crew members Zoro, Usopp, Sanji,and Nami. As your crew grows, your Max HP increases. On other islands, you encounter mini-bosses Buggy, Don Krieg, and Arlong. Finally, you reach the Grand Line, fight the Warlord Crocodile in Alabasta, and have to make a final choice to fight or flee the Marines.
